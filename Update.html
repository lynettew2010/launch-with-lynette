Automation Case Study

Automated applicant scoring, Gmail alerts, and source tracking for a mentorship offer

Tiffany needed a smarter way to review mentorship applicants and quickly identify who was the strongest fit. She also wanted clearer visibility into the sources bringing qualified leads to her application page.

Situation

Applications were coming in, but manually reviewing them slowed down decision-making and made it harder to prioritize follow-up with the best-fit candidates.

Solution

I helped build a workflow that used scoring logic on the website form, triggered Gmail notifications showing how applicants ranked, and captured the source that brought them to the page.

  • Applicant scoring logic built into the intake process
  • Gmail notifications with ranked applicants
  • Source tracking for incoming leads
  • Faster visibility into who to follow up with first

Outcome

Tiffany gained more control over her applicant pipeline, stronger visibility into lead quality, and a cleaner decision-making process for follow-up.

Prioritized Applicant ranking for faster follow-up
Tracked Lead source visibility from traffic
Streamlined Less manual sorting and review friction
